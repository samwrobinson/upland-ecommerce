From the Heart of Vermont

To Your Home.

Crafted with care and inspired by the rugged beauty of the Green Mountains, Upland Coffee is more than just a cup of coffee; it’s a Vermont tradition. We hand-roast each batch, blending sustainable practices with bold, distinctive flavors to bring you a coffee experience like no other.

Founded in 2022, Upland Coffee began as a winter project for founders Moxie and Sam Robinson after Moxie’s ACL injury put her mountain adventures on hold. Bonding over a shared passion, they discovered a love for crafting small-batch coffee that blossomed into Upland Coffee.

With backgrounds as diverse as Vermont itself, Moxie and Sam Robinson bring artistry, passion, and experience to each roast. From marketing to perfecting the roast, this duo has transformed Upland Coffee from a small project into a craft coffee brand that brings a taste of Vermont to you.
